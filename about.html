Hi, I'm Jenn Guneratne, the New England-based photographer behind Bosco Images Wildlife Photography. I studied digital photography in the early 2010s, but wildlife photography didn't find me until more than a decade later. The journey began at the edge of the woods in my local area, capturing small animals, birds and insects. I enjoy spending time outdoors, which allows me to observe the abundant wildlife that lives nearby.

Each gallery here displays photos according to the orientation of your device: either landscape or portrait view. If you enjoy what you see, you can rotate the screen to see more.
